# KURIKOL-5-360  

**KURIKOL-5:360** is a passionate hackathon team that participated in the **Design Spark Challenge 2025**,  
organized by **School of Design Thinking**, **Academia NXT**, **Intellect Design Arena**, and **NASSCOM**.  

---

### About the Hackathon  
- A **50-hour sleepless hackathon** that challenged innovation, design, and teamwork.  
- We developed **AgriConnect** — a digital platform empowering farmers through technology.  
- **Secured 2nd Runner-Up** position with a **cash prize of ₹40,000**.  
- A team of **five creative innovators** driven by purpose and passion for impact.  

---

### Our Project — AgriConnect  
**AgriConnect** bridges the gap between **farmers**, **consumers**, and **agricultural service providers**.  
It focuses on:  
- Connecting stakeholders like transporters, advisors, and financiers  
- Providing weather insights and government loan details
